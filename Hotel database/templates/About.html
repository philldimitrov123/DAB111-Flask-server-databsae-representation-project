About The Hotel Data

This site contains hotel booking data across various countries, The data is originally from the article Hotel Booking Demand Datasets, written by Nuno Antonio, Ana Almeida and Luis Nunes. The original data set contains information about bookings that were cancelled and ones that were not, we cleaned the data set to only include bookings that were not cancelled.

Variable Descriptions

  1. Hotel: Type of hotel that was booked, could be city hotel or resort hotel
  2. Country: Which country the stay was booked in, it is abreviated
  3. reservation_status_date: The date the reservation was booked
  4. total_nights_stayed: Total nights stayed
  5. adr: Average daily rate of the stay in dollars
  6. adults: Amount of adults
  7. children: Amount of children
  8. babies: Amount of babies
  9. required_parking_spaces: Total parking spaces required for the stay
  10. total_of_special_requests: Total special requests submitted during the stay
